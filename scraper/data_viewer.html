🎲 Contrarian Betting Strategy

  • Core Principle: Results are based on LOWEST betting volume (not random)
  • House Strategy: Selects unpopular numbers but ensures some winners exist
  • Our Approach: Find the "sweet spot" - numbers that are unpopular but not TOO unpopular
  • Avoid: Sequential patterns (123, 456), Lucky digits (7,8,9), Triple numbers (000, 111)
  • Target: Numbers with low historical frequency and no popular patterns
